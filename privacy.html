Privacy Policy & User Agreement

1. Data Architecture

LOTO PRO is built as a Local-First Application. This means that all facility names, equipment data, and site photos entered into the interface are processed exclusively within your browser's temporary memory (RAM). We do not operate a central database, and your proprietary industrial data is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Camera & Photo Usage

The application requires camera access to document energy isolation points.

  • Photos are stored in a local JavaScript object during your session.
  • Images are encoded as Base64 strings for the purpose of PDF generation only.
  • All image data is permanently destroyed when the "Reset" button is clicked or the browser tab is closed.

3. Compliance Responsibility

"OSHA 29 CFR 1910.147 requires that energy control procedures be developed, documented, and utilized. While LOTO PRO facilitates this documentation, the authorized user is solely responsible for verifying the accuracy of all isolation points, energy magnitudes, and verification steps."

4. External Libraries

We utilize third-party libraries (jsPDF, TailwindCSS, QRious) via CDNs. These services provide the functional code needed for layout and PDF creation. They do not have access to the data you input into the fields.
